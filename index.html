Google Sheets Setup Guide

Step 1 — Create your Google Sheet

Create a new Google Sheet with one tab per company, plus an Engagements tab:

  • Engagements — one row per company (overview)
  • Vetto AI — one row per talent for that company
  • Scale AI — same structure, named exactly as in Engagements
  • …one tab per company, named to match the Company column exactly

Step 2 — Add these column headers (Row 1)

Engagements tab:

Company | Status | Sourcing Required | Sourcing Status | Channel Link | Project Type | Notes | Target Talent | Onboarded | Pending | Risk Level | Risk Reason | Target Date | Ramp Up Plan | Last Updated

Each company tab (e.g. "Vetto AI"):

# | Talent | Role | Project | Date Added | Country | Language | Rate | Summary | Email

The Project column groups talent into tabs (e.g. "SWE", "Financial Advisors", "Terminal Bench"). Leave blank to show all talent in a single list.

Engagements Status values: Active Contracting Proposal Sales

Risk Level values: High Medium Low None

Step 3 — Make the sheet public

File → Share → Publish to web → Publish (keep default settings)

Step 4 — Get your Sheet ID

Copy the ID from your sheet URL:

docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1BxiMVs0XRA5nFMdKvBdBZjgmUUqptlbs74OgVE2upms/edit

Step 5 — Enter the Sheet ID in Config

Click the ⚙ Config button in the top nav, paste your Sheet ID, and click Save & Reload.
